Game

Choose which level of challenge you want. Each will provide a different degree of difficulty and will ask you to navigate through campus from a predetermined starting point to a predetermined ending point. If you get stumped, feel free to consult the links on the home page so you can get a better understanding of where each location is on campus! Each location will provide some trivia or history, as well as an image of the location and a Google Map centered on the location. Good luck!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What does this game do?

This game allows you to pick a starting point and a destination point make choices of how to get to your destination.


Q2: How do I start the game?

Choose a starting point and destination point in the homescreen then click the "Start" button.


Q3: How do I win?

You win when you successfully make it to the destination.
