Welcome to the Navigation Game

From here, choose which level of challenge you want. Each will provide a different degree of difficulty and will ask you to navigate through campus from a predetermined starting point to a predetermined ending point. If you get stumped, feel free to consult the links on the home page so you can get a better understanding of where each location is on campus! Each location will provide some trivia or history, as well as an image of the location and a Google Map centered on the location. Good luck!
