Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What does this game do?

This game allows you to pick a starting point and a destination point make choices of how to get to your destination.


Q2: How do I start the game?

Choose a starting point and destination point in the homescreen then click the "Start" button.


Q3: How do I win?

You win when you successfully make it to the destination.
