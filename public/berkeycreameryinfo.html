Welcome to Berkey Creamery!

To anyone with a sweet tooth on campus, Berkey Creamery should be no stranger. Famous for its ice cream and strict no mixing policy, Berkey Creamery serves an incredible quantity of ice cream yearly to students, parents, and visitors.

Some of the many flavors served at Berkey Creamery include Death by Chocolate, Alumni Swirl, Vanilla Bean, Butter Pecan, and Peachy Paterno. Berkey Creamery also ships to 48 states!
