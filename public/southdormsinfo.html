Welcome to South Dorms!

South Dorms are located along East College Avenue, making it an easy walk to downtown State College! South Housing Area offers 3200 spaces throughout the buildings for first-years and upperclassmen, with limited space for 890 first-year students at McElwain, Atherton, and Simmons Halls.

South also offers rennovated options, a computer lab, a cultural lounge, a food court, and a convienince store.
