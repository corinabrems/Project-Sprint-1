Welcome to Pattee & Paterno Library!

Students can access many resources on the library's website by logging in with their psu credentials! The library offers course reserves, interlibrary loans, an alumni library, virtual reality, and tech support/equipment.

Students can also reserve seats and rooms for studying or they can use the avaialble rooms and spaces to study. There is convieniently a Starbucks on the Pattee side of the libray by the study rooms.
