Welcome to the Bryce Jordan Center!

From its website, "Located on the campus of the Pennsylvania State University, the Bryce Jordan Center is central Pennsylvania’s premier entertainment facility. The 15,000+ seat arena is the only facility of its size in the region. Since its opening in 1996, the arena has hosted top-name performers in the music entertainment industry as well as family shows, sporting events, and commencements. The Bryce Jordan Center is also home to both Penn State’s Men’s and Women’s NCAA Division 1 Basketball Teams. In addition to arena bowl events, our flexible meeting space can accommodate meetings, training sessions, conferences, trade shows, banquets and more."
